About the Project

Welcome to AI Art - an interactive web application that showcases historic and current American Indian art and artists. We are interested in exploring the transmission of cultural material on the internet. As more and more people spend time on the internet, developments in technology allow novel art interactive experiences. This website can serve as an alternate market for many artists who have been impacted by COVID-19. We hope that this website will be educational and enjoyable. This web application was built during the summer of 2020 at the University of California, Santa Cruz.
