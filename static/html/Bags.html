Bag

CULTURE/PEOPLE: Meskwaki (Fox)

MEDIA/MATERIALS: Twined

OBJECT TYPE: Bags/Pouches (and parts)

PLACE: Iowa; USA

COLLECTION HISTORY: Items in this set were collected from several Meskwaki (Fox) individuals (Pe'-mi'-ta, Kwe'-ta-dji', Ke'-ke'-kwu'-mo'-ha, Me'c-ki'-gwe', Pe'-mi'-ka-wi'-kw, Me's-kwa-na-kwi', Chief Joe Tesson, Mr. and Mrs. Jim Scott, Me's-kwa-gu'n, A-yan-a-pi', Mu'-dji'-kwaw, Wa-ta-dji', Ne'-ka-pa, and Janie) in 1910 by anthropologist Mark Raymond Harrington (1882-1971, MAI staff member) during fieldwork sponsored by George Heye.
